Featured Case Study

Winning Search in the Age of AI Assistants

A three-layer visibility strategy for a national HVAC and air filtration brand.

Company Filterbuy, Inc.
Industry HVAC / Air Filtration
Role SEO Specialist — Content Operations
Scope National (US Market)

The Challenge

The HVAC and air filtration market seems deceptively simple: customers search for filter sizes, replacement schedules, and brand comparisons. But by 2024, the search landscape had fundamentally changed. Customers increasingly turned to AI assistants with questions like "how often should I replace my MERV 13 filter?" — and received a generated answer citing the sources AI models had learned to trust.

Traditional SEO alone was no longer enough. A brand could rank on Page 1 for every target keyword and still be invisible when a customer asked Perplexity, ChatGPT, or Google AI Mode for a recommendation. The challenge was building a presence not just in Google's index, but across the full spectrum of modern search — blue links, featured snippets, AI Overviews, and generative responses.

The Strategy

Three Layers. One Unified Presence.

Each discipline plays a distinct role — and they compound each other when applied together.

Layer 1

Search Engine Optimization

The foundation. Visibility in Google's organic index.

  • Keyword mapping across size-specific queries (e.g. "16x25x4 MERV 13 air filter"), replacement intent, and head-to-head comparisons
  • Content hub architecture — filter size pages cross-linked to MERV guides, symptom articles, and brand pages
  • On-page optimization: title tags, meta descriptions, header hierarchy, semantic keyword density
  • FAQ and HowTo schema markup to qualify pages for rich results
  • Off-page authority through author profiles on IndoorAirHub.com and HVACLoop.com
  • Pinterest brand presence supporting visual SEO and product entity signals
Layer 2

Answer Engine Optimization

The bridge. Owning the answer box, not just the link.

  • Every article structured with a direct, concise answer paragraph (under 60 words) before expanding into detail — giving search engines exactly what they need to surface an answer
  • Targeted People Also Ask boxes: "what does MERV rating mean," "how to check if your air filter needs replacing," "what happens if you don't change your air filter"
  • Structured FAQ schema on high-traffic informational pages to qualify for Google's featured snippet and PAA placement
  • Definitional content built to appear in Knowledge Panels — tying the brand to core HVAC entities
  • Voice-search-ready phrasing for conversational queries from smart home assistants
Layer 3

Generative Engine Optimization

The future layer. Being cited by AI, not just ranked by Google.

  • Content structured for AI citability: clear author attribution, expert positioning, original claims backed by verifiable data — the signals AI models use to determine trustworthiness
  • Published on authoritative third-party domains (IndoorAirHub, HVACLoop) to create expert nodes that AI models associate with HVAC topics
  • Entity-first content architecture: brand name + product category + specific verifiable claim — the building block of AI citations
  • Tested and confirmed: content surfacing in Google AI Mode, Perplexity, and ChatGPT search results for target HVAC queries
  • E-E-A-T signals reinforced through consistent author identity, domain specialization, and cross-platform presence

The Argument

Why all three disciplines must work together

SEO Alone

You rank in the index. But when a customer asks Perplexity or ChatGPT for a recommendation, your page is bypassed entirely. AI search answers the question — your blue link never gets clicked.

AEO Alone

You appear in featured snippets and PAA boxes. But without the entity authority, author trust signals, and citable structure that AI models require, you remain invisible in generative results — the fastest-growing search surface.

GEO Alone

AI tools cite your content. But traditional search still accounts for the majority of search volume and commercial intent clicks. Ignoring the index means ignoring your highest-converting traffic channel.

All Three

SEO builds the foundation. AEO captures the zero-click moment. GEO earns a place in the AI's reasoning. Together, they make a brand the trusted answer — regardless of how the customer chooses to search.

“

SEO gets you into the index. AEO gets you into the answer box. GEO gets you into the AI's reasoning. A brand that masters all three doesn't just rank — it becomes the answer.

— Core principle applied at Filterbuy, Inc.
